Core Focus Areas

Targeted interventions where we create measurable, scalable impact

Education Access

Our Global Learning Initiative has established 1,200 community learning hubs across 38 countries, providing localized education solutions that combine digital tools with in-person mentorship.

  • Digital literacy programs reaching 250,000+ annually
  • Teacher training in 15 languages
  • Open-source curriculum adaptable to local contexts

Women & Girls Empowerment

The Rising Feathers program creates ecosystems where women and girls can thrive through education, leadership training, and economic opportunities.

  • 92% high school completion rate for participants
  • Micro-enterprise incubators in 24 countries
  • Global mentorship network with 8,000+ professionals
Explore Empowerment Programs ‚Üí

Community-Led Development

We mobilize citizens across the African Continent to advocate for policy changes that address root causes of inequality, not just symptoms.

  • 142 active policy campaigns across 6 cities
  • Digital advocacy training in 11 languages
  • UN ECOSOC special consultative status
